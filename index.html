Our Story

Why Us

Motivated Military Movers was founded in 2008 by a Navy Corpsman. The goal from the beginning was always to save customers the most time and money on their move while protecting their belongings. Its not been a easy thing to do; but MMM has been passionate about accomplishing this and has always held the belief of quality over quantity

Motivated Military Movers is more than just a Moving company, We are a Veteran owned organization with a goal to hire a 100% Military & Veterans. Transitioning from the Military can be a difficult process; Being around other Veterans, getting immediate employment while learning a new trade and using your military skills & traits makes it much easier getting out. Its also rewarding for our customers that hire MMM as moving is one of the most stressful things you can do in life, When you have Movers that are professional, disciplined and trustworthy moving can be a fun, not dreadful thing to do.
